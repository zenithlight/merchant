How to play: click a tile to remove it from the board. You will recieve as much money as it says on the bottom-right of the tile. Tiles can have special effects that are described below.

Tiles:
Brown: Bread, gives you +3 money.
Gold: Gold Bar, gains +2 value each turn.
Turquoise: Diamond, loses -3 value each turn. Doubles your money.
White: Yarn, increases the value of adjacent tiles by 5.
